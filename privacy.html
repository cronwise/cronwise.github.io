Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

The short version

Cronwise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens locally on your Mac.

Data collection

Cronwise collects no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports are sent
  • No cookies or tracking of any kind
  • No accounts or sign-ins

Network access

Cronwise makes zero network requests. The app runs entirely offline. It does not connect to any server, API, or third-party service.

Local storage

Cronwise stores a single preference (your chosen cron format: 5-field or 6-field) using macOS UserDefaults. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-party services

Cronwise uses no third-party SDKs, frameworks, or services. It is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
